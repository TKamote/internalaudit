FSM Monthly Inspection Report

Fire Safety Inspection Checklist

A. PROTECTED AREA / CORRIDORS / LOBBIES / PASSAGEWAYS / STAIRCASES
S/No Description Status
i. Such areas are clear of obstruction
ii. Services pipes (gas, oil) are not passing through the protected zones
iii. Escape routes plans are provided at the lobbies
B. ESCAPE ROUTES
S/No Description Status
i. Exit signs and directional signs are sufficiently provided
ii. Final exit discharge points not obstructed
iii. Door release devices are in good working condition
iv. The lightings and illumination of staircases are good.
v. The passageways and office configuration are maintained with at least 1.2m clearance
C. FIRE DOORS
S/No Description Status
i. Door closers are provided and are in good condition
ii. PSB labels provided for all fire doors and frames
iii. No door stopper devices installed
iv. Doors are close-fitting.
v. Swing of doors in direction of escape
vi. Clear of obstruction
vii. Vision panels are provided
viii. Fire doors are not locked
ix. Door handles, door closers, door bodies are in good condition
D. EMERGENCY / EXIT AND LIGHTING
S/No Description Status
i. Exit signs and emergency lighting adequately provided along escape routes, public areas, staircase, and essential services areas
ii. Exit signs are visible
iii. Exit signs are functioning well
E. HOSEREELS
S/No Description Status
i. Nozzle condition satisfactory
ii. Stopcock condition satisfactory
iii. Clear of obstruction
iv. Servicing labeling provided for cabinet
v. Date service is shown and has not expired
F. FIRE EXTINGUISHERS
S/No Description Status
i. Properly hung on bracket/ inside labeled cabinet
ii. Date service is shown and has not expired
iii. Clear of obstruction
iv. Bears SISIR label
v. Pressure gauge of Fire Extinguishers within functional range
vi. Fire Extinguishers seal not broken
vii. Fire Extinguishers body not rusty
G. HYDRANT
S/No Description Status
i. Clear of obstruction
ii. Cover for spindle chamber visible
iii. Blank caps provided to outlets
H. Dry Riser Breeching Inlet / Dry Riser Landing Valves
S/No Description Status
i. Clear of dirt or corrosion
ii. Clear of obstruction or blockage to the inlets
iii. Valves are strap-locked
iv. Compartment free of foreign objects
I. Fire Command Centre
S/No Description Status
i. PA system functioning
ii. Battery working properly
iii. Lift panel control functioning
J. Main / Sub Fire Alarm panel
S/No Description Status
i. Battery working properly
ii. All LED lights working - lamp test
iii. Any alerts (warnings), alarms or faults indicated in the panel
K. Fireman Access Panel, Fire Engine & Fire Hydrant
S/No Description Status
i. No obstruction to Fire men accesses panel windows
ii. No obstruction to fire engine parking lot
